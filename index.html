Features

Welcome to Farming Paradise

Make RP your way—most efficient via farming. Sell crops at the Community Center using /warp. Use /info anytime to reopen the info UI.

Wipe Schedule: Map wipes the 1st & 3rd Thursday each month at 2PM EST. Blueprints wipe every Force.

Farmer Point Tiers

Seed Sower (≤1k) • Farmer (1k–5k) • Pro Farmer (5k–10k) • Farm Master (10k–25k) • Elite (25k–50k) • Diamond (50k+).

Track progress with /stats, and invest XP in buffs via /st (Skilltree).

Heli Signals

Buy patrol helicopters in multiple difficulties to fight at your base. They cannot damage your base.

Bosses

Map-roaming bosses (~5,000 HP) that reward RP on kill.

World Events

Convoy, Supermarket, Gas Station, Armored Train, and more. Events reward RP; buyables do not.

Survival Arena

Battle Royale—last player standing wins. When you see it announced in chat, type /survival to join.

Zombie Infection

Survivors vs Zombies. A random player becomes Zombie Prime with high HP. Two zombie hits convert survivors. Survive until the timer expires.

Ganja Buffs

Low Joint → Wood boost • Medium Joint → Ore boost • High Joint → Scrap boost.

Growing & Crafting

H genes decide quality: 4H=Low • 5H=Low+Medium • 6H=Low+Medium+High. Roll joints at a Mixing Table → “Show Custom Recipes”.

Water & Pumps

“Place anywhere” water pumps: /pump (help) and /pump craft to craft.

Raidable Bases (Buyables)

Check price with /raidcost and start with /buyraid. View raiding stats via /rb.

Abandoned Bases

After 5 days offline, bases become raidable to all. These are PVP zones—if you die inside, you lose your inventory.

Defendable Homes — Build a separate base for these. They can foundation‑wipe and are tough.
