// What's on the Server
FEATURES
🌾
FARMING & ECONOMY
Farm crops, craft Ganja joints for buffs (wood, ore, scrap), and sell at the Community Center via /warp. Earn RP and climb Farmer Point tiers from Seed Sower to Diamond.
🚁
HELI SIGNALS
Buy patrol helicopters in multiple difficulties that fight at your base. They cannot damage your base — pure combat challenge for RP rewards.
💀
BOSSES
Map-roaming bosses with ~5,000 HP that reward RP on kill. Track them down and take them out for big payouts.
🎯
WORLD EVENTS
Convoy, Supermarket, Gas Station, Armored Train, and more. Events reward RP — coordinate with others or solo them.
⚔️
RAIDABLE BASES
Buy raids with /buyraid, check cost with /raidcost. Abandoned bases (5 days offline) go public — PVP zones where death means losing your inventory.
🎮
MINIGAMES
Survival Arena (Battle Royale) and Zombie Infection. Watch for announcements in chat and jump in — last one standing wins.
🎨
FREE SKINBOX
Full skinbox access for every player at no cost. Use /sb to skin items, /sd for placed items, or /skinrequest for workshop skins by ID.
📈
SKILLTREE
Invest XP into buffs via /st. Track farming stats with /stats and playtime with /playtime. Progress means something here.

// Reference
COMMANDS
// Core
  • /tp Teleport GUI
  • /warp Warp to Community Center & more
  • /voteday Vote to skip night
  • /s Store UI
  • /transfer Send RP to another player
  • /trade <player> Open trade with player
  • /remove Demolish within TC range
  • /info Reopen the info UI
// Teleport
  • /sethome <name> Set a home point
  • /home <name> Teleport to home
  • /listhomes List all homes
  • /delhome <name> Delete a home
  • /tpr <player> Request teleport to player
  • /tpa <name> Accept a teleport request
// Farming & Progression
  • /st Skilltree — invest XP into buffs
  • /stats View your farming stats
  • /playtime View your playtime
  • /playtime top Server playtime leaderboard
  • /pump Anywhere Pump help
  • /pump craft Craft an Anywhere Pump
// Skins
  • /sb Open Skinbox
  • /sd Skin placed items
  • /skinrequest Request workshop skin by ID
  • /sil <url> Set sign image from URL
  • /silrestore Restore sign image
// Raiding
  • /raidcost Check current raid price
  • /buyraid Purchase a raidable base
  • /rb View your raiding stats
// Misc
  • /survival Join Survival Arena when announced
  • /lockmini Lock your minicopter
  • /rspawn mini Spawn mini (VIP III)
